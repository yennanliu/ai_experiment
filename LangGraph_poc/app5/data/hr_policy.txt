HR Policy Manual — Acme Corp (2024)

## Leave Policy

Annual Leave
Employees are entitled to 15 days of annual leave per calendar year. Annual leave accrues at 1.25 days per month. Unused leave up to 5 days may be carried over to the following year. Leave requests must be submitted at least 3 business days in advance via the HR portal.

Sick Leave
Employees receive 10 days of paid sick leave per year. A medical certificate is required for absences exceeding 2 consecutive days. Sick leave does not carry over to the following year.

Parental Leave
Primary caregivers are entitled to 16 weeks of paid parental leave. Secondary caregivers receive 4 weeks of paid parental leave. Leave must commence within 12 months of the child's birth or adoption.

## Remote Work Policy

Employees may work remotely up to 3 days per week with manager approval. Remote workers must be available during core hours: 10:00 AM – 3:00 PM local time. A dedicated, distraction-free workspace is required. All remote work must comply with the company's data security guidelines.

## Performance Review Process

Reviews are conducted twice per year: mid-year (June) and end-of-year (December). The review process includes self-assessment, manager assessment, and a 1:1 discussion. Performance is rated on a 5-point scale: Exceptional, Exceeds Expectations, Meets Expectations, Needs Improvement, Unsatisfactory. Employees rated "Needs Improvement" for two consecutive cycles will enter a Performance Improvement Plan (PIP).

## Code of Conduct

All employees must treat colleagues, clients, and partners with respect and professionalism. Harassment, discrimination, and retaliation of any kind are strictly prohibited. Violations should be reported to HR or via the anonymous ethics hotline.

## Expense Reimbursement

Business expenses up to $500 may be approved by the direct manager. Expenses above $500 require VP approval. All claims must be submitted within 30 days of the expense with original receipts. Travel expenses follow the corporate travel policy (economy class for flights under 6 hours).
