# AKA `Cost Recovery Swing System`

> Please analyze my stock holdings using tax-lot level precision.
>
> I want a **professional portfolio optimization strategy focused on:
> (1) Free carry conversion
> (2) Break-even capital recovery
> (3) Volatility-based swing trading strategy after cost recovery**
>
> ---
>
> ### 📊 Inputs:
>
> I will provide my transaction-level cost basis / lots and current market value.
>
> ---
>
> ### 🎯 Required analysis:
>
> 1. **Summarize total position**
>
>    * total shares
>    * total cost basis
>    * unrealized gain/loss
>    * weighted average cost
> 2. **Lot decomposition analysis**
>
>    * group lots into:
>
>      * low-cost core (winning positions)
>      * mid-cost
>      * high-cost recent additions
> 3. **Free carry calculation**
>
>    * calculate exact number of shares needed to sell to fully recover total cost basis
>    * identify which specific lots should be sold first
>    * prioritize selling high-cost / low-conviction lots
> 4. **Optimal execution plan**
>
>    * step-by-step sell plan (not just theory)
>    * exact lots and quantities to sell
>    * whether to split orders or execute in batches
> 5. **Post free-carry structure**
>
>    * remaining shares after cost recovery
>    * effective “risk-free position size”
>    * psychological exposure after adjustment
> 6. **Volatility trading strategy after free carry**
>
>    * define support / resistance zones
>    * when to add back shares
>    * when to trim positions
>    * swing trading logic using realized gains only
> 7. **Risk assessment**
>
>    * concentration risk
>    * downside exposure
>    * whether position is suitable for long-term hold vs trading asset
>
