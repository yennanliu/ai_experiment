Meta Careers
Position: Backend Engineer
Location: Menlo Park, CA | Remote (US)
Job Type: Full-time

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
About the Role
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Meta is seeking experienced Backend Engineers to join our team. As a Backend Engineer, you will be responsible for building scalable infrastructure and services to power Meta’s family of apps including Facebook, Instagram, and WhatsApp. You’ll work on large-scale distributed systems, storage solutions, and real-time data pipelines that support billions of users.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Responsibilities
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
- Design and implement backend systems and APIs that power our products.
- Optimize performance and scalability of backend services.
- Collaborate cross-functionally with frontend, product, and data teams.
- Write clean, testable, and efficient code following software engineering best practices.
- Participate in code reviews and contribute to team knowledge-sharing.
- Monitor, debug, and improve existing systems.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Minimum Qualifications
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
- Bachelor’s or Master’s degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or related field.
- 3+ years of experience in backend development.
- Proficiency in one or more backend languages: Java, C++, Python, Go.
- Experience with relational and NoSQL databases (e.g., MySQL, Cassandra, RocksDB).
- Strong understanding of distributed systems and microservices architecture.
- Familiarity with containerization and orchestration tools like Docker and Kubernetes.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Preferred Qualifications
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
- Experience with large-scale systems, high-throughput APIs, or real-time systems.
- Knowledge of caching strategies using Memcached or Redis.
- Experience in data pipeline development using Kafka or similar tools.
- Open-source contributions or experience with open-source projects.
