Uber Careers
Position: Backend Software Engineer
Location: San Francisco, CA | Remote Eligible (US)
Job Type: Full-time

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
About the Role
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
At Uber, Backend Engineers build the systems that power the world’s largest mobility platform. You’ll work on high-scale distributed systems that handle real-time transportation, logistics, payments, and more. This is an opportunity to solve complex engineering challenges that have a direct impact on millions of users across the globe.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
What You’ll Do
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
- Design, develop, test, and deploy backend systems and services with high availability and scalability.
- Collaborate closely with Product, Data, and Frontend teams to deliver end-to-end features.
- Write clean, efficient, and maintainable code using industry best practices.
- Drive engineering excellence through code reviews, system design discussions, and mentoring.
- Monitor, troubleshoot, and improve system performance and reliability.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Minimum Qualifications
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
- Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or a related field (or equivalent practical experience).
- 3+ years of experience building backend systems or microservices.
- Strong proficiency in at least one backend language (Go, Java, Python, or Node.js).
- Experience with scalable system architecture, RESTful APIs, and cloud-native development.
- Familiarity with SQL and NoSQL databases such as PostgreSQL, Cassandra, or DynamoDB.
- Knowledge of CI/CD pipelines and DevOps tooling.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Preferred Qualifications
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
- Experience working in high-growth tech environments.
- Hands-on experience with event-driven architecture using Kafka or similar technologies.
- Familiarity with containerized environments (Docker, Kubernetes).
- Exposure to observability tools like Prometheus, Grafana, or Datadog.
- Contributions to open-source projects or tech blogs are a plus.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Perks & Benefits

- Competitive salary and performance-based bonuses.
- RSU (Restricted Stock Unit) grants.
- Comprehensive healthcare coverage (medical, dental, vision).
- Generous parental and family leave.
- Uber credits and monthly wellness stipend.
- Flexible hours and hybrid work options.
